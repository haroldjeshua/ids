Typography

IntegrityUI provides a constrained, purposeful set of typographic styles. These styles map to functional roles so you know when to use what.

Typeface:

Maison Neue

Maison Neue is a grotesque sans-serif typeface designed by Timo Gaessner and released through Milieu Grotesque in 2012. It's a complete reworking of the original Maison, with updates made to take advantage of new display and reproduction technologies.

Design

Iconography

Icons in the IntegrityUI act as visual aids to help users understand better and complete tasks. They’re simple, informative, and build on the visual language of the design system.

And icons can be divided into multiple groups for different purposes:

  • Product Icons
  • System Icons
  • Animated Icons

Product Icons

Product icons are the visual expression of a brand’s products, services, and tools.

Often used icon resources:
  • icons8 (https://icons8.com/icons)

System Icons

Their purpose is to represent certain actions on the website or platform

Often used icons library:
  • Material Icons (https://materialdesignicons.com/)
  • Line Awesome (https://icons8.com/line-awesome)
