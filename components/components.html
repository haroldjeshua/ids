Buttons: Modifiers and Utilities

IntegrityUI Buttons have different modifiers for every occasions:

  • Contained (Primary)
  • Outline
  • Modern
  • Link
  • Flat / Raised
  • with Icons

Utilities that may come in handy in building components:

  • Alerts
  • Rounds
  • Sizes
  • Special
  • For Mobile App
